The Challenges We Address

India's agricultural sector forms the economic bedrock, employing nearly 43% of the nation's workforce. Yet small and marginal farmers face critical challenges:

  • Low incomes: Average earnings as low as ₹816.5 per person per month
  • Information gap: Lack of timely data on weather, soil health, and market prices
  • Limited access: To modern equipment, credit, and fair markets
  • Post-harvest losses: Up to 40% wastage due to inefficient supply chains
  • Climate vulnerability: Increasing challenges from changing weather patterns

Our Unique Approach

Phygital Strategy

We blend digital tools with physical presence through village-level field agents and Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs). This hybrid approach bridges the digital literacy gap and builds trust in rural communities.

Farmer-Centric Design

Our solutions are built with smallholder realities in mind - low data usage apps, vernacular interfaces, and flexible payment models that align with harvest cycles.

Integrated Ecosystem

We address the entire agricultural value chain - from farm inputs and cultivation to market access and waste management - creating a holistic solution for farmers.
